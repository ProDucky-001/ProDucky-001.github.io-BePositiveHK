Meet Our Team

As we embark on the journey to raise mental health awareness, the Be Positive HK Executive Committee is determined to make a positive impact in Hong Kong. Our 2026–2027 structure includes an Executive Committee, Advisory Board, and dedicated teams across events, media, research, and interviews.

Our Team Members · 2026–2027

Executive Committee

Ms Charlotte Yuen

Founder & Co-Director

Psychology, The University of Sydney · Graduate, Maryknoll Convent School

Hello everyone, I am Charlotte Yuen, the founder of Be Positive HK. When I was 16, I founded Be Positive HK in 2023, a student-led initiative which aims to promote mental well-being, as well as encouraging teenagers who are struggling with mental health issues based on my own experiences. I hope to empower individuals by creating safe spaces for open dialogue, support, and education around mental health issues. I also believe that by sharing experiences and knowledge, we can break down stigma and encourage a culture of understanding and empathy.

Advisory Board

Ms Michelle Li

Honorary Advisor · Educational Psychologist

Registered Educational Psychologist · Vice President, The Hong Kong Psychological Society · Director, HKSPI

Ms Michelle Li is a dedicated professional in the field of educational psychology in Hong Kong. She holds a Bachelor of Arts with Honours in Natural Sciences (Psychology) from the University of Cambridge, a Master of Social Sciences in Educational Psychology (Distinction), and a Postgraduate Diploma in Education (Chinese), both from The University of Hong Kong. She is a Registered Educational Psychologist with extensive experience supporting children and adolescents with special educational needs and mental health challenges, as well as working closely with their families and schools. She currently serves as Vice President and Associate Fellow of the Hong Kong Psychological Society. In addition, she is the Director and Honorary Advisor of the Hong Kong Society of Psychological Innovation.

Operations Advisor

Michael Chan is a registered social worker and a brain cancer survivor. Ms Sophie Lee

Legal Advisor

BA & LLB, The University of Hong Kong

Sophie Lee is an undergraduate student pursuing a Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Laws (BA&LLB) at The University of Hong Kong, with a strong academic foundation and a keen interest in interdisciplinary research that bridges legal, cultural, psychological and social dimensions.
