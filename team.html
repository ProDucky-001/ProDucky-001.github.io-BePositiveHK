Meet Our Team

As we embark on the journey to raise mental health awareness, the Be Positive HK Executive Committee is determined to make a positive impact in Hong Kong. Our 2026–2027 structure includes an Executive Committee, Advisory Board, and dedicated teams across events, media, research, and interviews.

Our Team Members · 2026–2027

Executive Committee

Charlotte Yuen

Founder & Co-Director

Psychology, The University of Sydney · Graduate, Maryknoll Convent School

Hello everyone, I am Charlotte, the founder of Be Positive HK! I hope to advocate for and raise public awareness of mental well-being, which led me to establish this student-led initiative. Our team aims to make a positive impact on society and bring comfort to those who are struggling mentally. Let's build a compassionate, supportive community that prioritises mental well-being!

Angus Lai

Co-Director

Hello everyone, I am Angus Lai, and I am one of the Co-Directors at Be Positive HK. Hong Kong's education system can be highly intense and stressful. Through activities with various stakeholders, our aim is to promote effective strategies for stress relief and to support the importance of maintaining good mental health.
