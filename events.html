Upcoming Interview Guests

勵志訪問系列 — new episodes in production for 2026.

Dr. Stephanie Ng-Cusi

Episode 7 · Interview recorded 30 April

Dr. Stephanie Ng-Cusi, Ph.D., is a multi-award-winning mental health advocate, edupreneur, author, and researcher whose work aims to foster caring conversations on body image and mental health issues. She is the founder of Body Banter, a registered Section 88 charity in Hong Kong on a mission to empower young people to spark conversations about body image with curiosity and courage. She is also the author of Big Bites Break Boundaries, a memoir that recounts her personal experiences with an eating disorder and her professional experiences in Hong Kong's mental health landscape. Her work has gained local and international recognition, impacting over 25,000 individuals to date.

Ms Gianne Chan

Episode 8

Gianne Chan is a rising force within Hong Kong's wellness and creative spaces, bringing her intentional approach to the forefront of the local holistic community. An advocate, singer-songwriter, podcaster, and the founder of multiple purpose-driven brands—including Calmed & Co., Lexemedia Creative Solutions, and the Let It 9 Podcast—Gianne plays many diverse roles in her everyday life. She has also partnered with homegrown non-profit organisation Inspiring Girls to provide career development opportunities and empower the future generation of women. Her transformative work within the space was steered by Gianne's battle with depression, as she strives to build a positive narrative around mental health, inspire others to navigate their emotional layers, and advocate for holistic wellbeing for all.
