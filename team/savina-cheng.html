Ms Savina Cheng

Academia Advisor

BSc Psychology, University College London

Savina is a graduate BSc Psychology student at UCL aspiring to become a clinical psychologist. She is particularly interested in emotional dysregulation and its interaction with psychopathy. With a strong academic background in psychology, she hopes to develop accessible tools that improve public mental health literacy and strengthen emotional resilience over time.
