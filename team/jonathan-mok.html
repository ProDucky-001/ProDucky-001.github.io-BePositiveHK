Mr Jonathan Mok

Operations Advisor

Global Neurodiversity Strategist · Organising Committee, Davos Neurodiversity Summit

Jonathan Mok is a Hong Kong-based TEDx Speaker, Neurodiversity Keynote Speaker for Asia, and an Executive ADHD Coach. As a UK Chartered Manager (CMgr) and an Organising Committee Member of the Davos Neurodiversity Summit, he is deeply committed to advancing neurodiversity inclusion. With extensive experience partnering with multinational corporations, regional enterprises, and nonprofits, Jonathan delivers measurable neurodiversity inclusion outcomes through corporate workshops, DEI consulting for APAC, and executive ADHD coaching. He aims to empower individuals and organizations to embrace neurodiversity and foster inclusive environments.
