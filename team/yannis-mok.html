Ms Yannis Mok

Academia Advisor

Jardine Scholar · Psychology & Linguistics, University of Oxford

Yannis is a MSci Psychology and Linguistics student at the University of Oxford. She is passionate about social impact and has been involved in various community initiatives: to promote education equality, she served as co-director of Access Abroad Hong Kong, a charity dedicated to widening access to overseas tertiary education, in 2025; to promote the mental health of seniors, she developed Teatre, a board game with the theme of Cantonese opera and Chinese tea to prevent cognitive decline and reduce loneliness among seniors.
