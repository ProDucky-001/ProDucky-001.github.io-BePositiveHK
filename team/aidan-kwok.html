Mr Aidan Kwok

Research Team Member

Pharmacy, The University of Sydney

Hi, I am Aidan Kwok, a Year 1 Pharmacy Student at the University of Sydney, and I'm so excited to be part of Be Positive HK's Research Team! I believe a lot of the mental health challenges we see in our community come down to people simply not knowing enough about it — and that's exactly what I want to help change. I'm really looking forward to taking research findings and turning them into something people can actually relate to and use in real life.
