Ms Sophie Lee

Legal Advisor

BA & LLB, The University of Hong Kong

Sophie Lee is an undergraduate student pursuing a Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Laws (BA&LLB) at The University of Hong Kong, with a strong academic foundation and a keen interest in interdisciplinary research that bridges legal, cultural, psychological and social dimensions. Her advocacy includes youth-led efforts on mental health and student wellbeing as a Member and Youth Ambassador for the Hong Kong Student Alliance on Student Suicide (HKSASS), where she has presented at youth summits on related educational topics. Sophie's dedication to promoting inclusion and social equity has earned recognition through prestigious awards, including the Zonta Club of Hong Kong Golden Z Outstanding Achievement Award; the Sensational Foundation Outstanding Youth Token of Recognition 2025; the Gold Student Award from the Law Society of Hong Kong's Pro Bono and Community Work Recognition Programme 2025; and selection as one of the top 20 university students in the Hong Kong Ming De Association 2025 Outstanding University Students Selection.
