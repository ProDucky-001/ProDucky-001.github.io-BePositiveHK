Mr Michael Chan

Operations Advisor

Founder, Companion HK · MSSc Social Work, CUHK

Michael Chan is a registered social worker and a brain cancer survivor. He believes everyone has the ability to contribute in society and founded Companion HK, a non-profit organization, at the peak of the pandemic in 2020 to help people with psychological ailments, empowering them to serve others and reintegrate into society. For helping reduce stigma surrounding mental disorders, Michael Chan is a honoree of Forbes Asia 30 Under 30, Tatler Hong Kong Generation T, Spirit of Hong Kong Award, and Hong Kong Humanity Youth Power Award.
