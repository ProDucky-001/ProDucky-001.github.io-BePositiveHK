About Us

Founded in 2023 by Charlotte Yuen, Be Positive HK is a student-led initiative that promotes mental well-being and supports secondary school students facing mental health challenges. Through workshops with NGOs, positive psychology programmes, and open dialogue, we create safe spaces where young people can learn, share, and feel understood.

成立於2023年，Be Positive HK 是一個致力於推廣精神健康的學生組織， 而我們的願景為創建一個關注精神健康的共融社會。

Our founder discovered two major gaps in Hong Kong’s mental health landscape: treatments are often not tailored to youth, and stigma still prevents many from seeking help. Be Positive HK works to close those gaps—empowering students through education, peer support, and authentic storytelling that builds empathy and reduces stigma.
