About Us

Founded in November 2023, Be Positive HK is a student-led initiative which aims to raise awareness about mental health and to create a compassionate community that prioritises mental well-being.

成立於2023年，Be Positive HK 是一個致力於推廣精神健康的學生組織， 而我們的願景為創建一個關注精神健康的共融社會。
